    Smart Send transfers are protected by data encryption. However, it is important to note that when using Smart Send, the onus of protecting your money falls on you, the user. Smart Send recommends that you think of the Smart Send service like cash: Send money only to people you trust, make sure your recipient’s contact information is correct before you send money, and beware of scams. Smart Send also provides authentication and monitoring features to help make your payments secure when you’re sending and receiving money.
